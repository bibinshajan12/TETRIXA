# Privacy Policy for TETRIXA

**Last updated:** December 19, 2024

TETRIXA ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application.

## Data Collection

**TETRIXA does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information.**

Our app operates entirely offline and does not require an internet connection to function. We do not collect any data about you or your device usage.

## Permissions

### Vibration Permission

TETRIXA requests the **VIBRATE** permission to provide haptic feedback during gameplay. This enhances the user experience by providing tactile feedback when pieces are moved or rotated.

- **Purpose**: Haptic feedback during gameplay
- **Data Collection**: None - vibration is used locally on your device only
- **Data Transmission**: No data is collected or transmitted

## Local Storage

TETRIXA stores game settings locally on your device using Android's SharedPreferences system. This includes:

- Grid size preferences
- Game speed settings
- Other gameplay preferences

**Important**: 
- This data is stored **only on your device**
- This data **never leaves your device**
- This data is **not shared** with us or any third parties
- You can clear this data by uninstalling the app

## Third-Party Services

TETRIXA does **not** use any:
- Analytics services
- Advertising networks
- Tracking services
- Social media integrations
- Cloud storage services
- Third-party data processors

## Children's Privacy

TETRIXA is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Since we do not collect any personal information, this is not applicable.
